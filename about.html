Who we are

About MCAC

A student-run aerospace club at Mira Costa High School in Manhattan Beach, CA.

Our story

Built from the ground up

The Mira Costa Aerospace Club was founded by a group of students who wanted more than classroom theory — they wanted to actually build things and send them into the sky. What started as an after-school group building Estes kits has grown into one of the most active student aerospace programs in the South Bay.

Today MCAC has three active divisions: Rocketry (in partnership with the Hermosa Beach Rocketry Club), Drone & UAV, and Research. Every division is entirely student-led, with faculty and community mentors offering guidance.

We believe the best way to learn aerospace engineering is to do aerospace engineering. Every member builds something real, launches something real, and learns from both the successes and the failures — usually spectacular ones.

What drives us

Our values

Four principles that shape everything we build.

01

Learn by doing

No passive learning here. Every member builds, launches, and iterates from day one.

02

Fail forward

Bad launches teach better lessons than perfect simulations. We embrace every CATO and hard landing.

03

Collaborate

Aerospace is a team sport. We mentor each other, share knowledge, and celebrate wins together.

04

Give back

We run youth workshops and outreach events so the next generation catches the aerospace bug too.

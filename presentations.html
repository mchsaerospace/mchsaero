📎 How to add a presentation

1

Export your presentation as a PDF — from Google Slides, PowerPoint, or Keynote. PDF works best for web display.

2

Upload it to GitHub — create a presentations/ folder in your repo and upload the PDF there (e.g. presentations/tarc-2025.pdf).

3

Copy one of the cards below and update the title, description, date, and the href on the download link to point to your file path (e.g. href="presentations/tarc-2025.pdf").

4

Optional: Take a screenshot of your first slide and save it as images/pres-tarc-2025.jpg, then replace the emoji in .pres-thumb with an <img> tag for a real preview.

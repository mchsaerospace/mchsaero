Rocketry

Build rockets that actually fly.

Members design airframes, assemble motors, wire recovery electronics, and fly at Lucerne Dry Lake. Every step — from paper design to launch day — is done by students.

We compete in TARC and NRC, two of the most prestigious high school rocketry competitions in the country.

Simulation & CAD

Engineer it before you build it.

We run computational fluid dynamics (CFD) simulations in SimScale to analyze aerodynamics and structural forces before a single part is cut.

CAD modeling lets us iterate fast and catch problems early — the same workflow used in real aerospace programs.

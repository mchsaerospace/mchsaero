❓ FAQ

Do I need experience?

Nope. We teach everything — rocket construction, OpenRocket, SimScale, CAD — from scratch.

Is there a cost?

Membership is free. Some launch trips may have a small fee for transport and range fees.

What competitions do you enter?

TARC (Team America Rocketry Challenge) and NRC (National Rocketry Competition).

Can I join mid-year?

Yes — new members are welcome any time during the school year.
